About

Quaesitor is a one of kind event that takes two stages to complete. The event is an innovative take on the general concept of idea pitching contests, treasure hunts and rapid fire quiz competitions. We aim to combine the three effectively, to create a platform that is enjoyable as well as academically useful for participant from all fields of engineering.

All resources regarding this event will be hosted on our website as well as our IEEE Student Branch's instagram handle, @ieeersetsb.

Guidelines

  1. The competition shall start on 23rd April, 2020 at 9:00 a.m.
  2. The event will be conducted in 2 sections. 1st section comprises of 4 problem statements and consecutive idea pitching sessions. The 2nd section consists of a rapid fire quiz competition.
  3. The problem statements will be posted in the Instagram page of the IEEE student branch of Rajagiri School of Engineering and technology. The Instagram handle is @ieeersetsb.
  4. Details for the rapid fire session will be released when the 1st section is over.
  5. The timing will be from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm. Each problem statement carries 10 marks and the criteria of evaluation will be based on practicality and effectiveness of the provided solution.
  6. The participants must submit the solution to the problem statement within 24hrs or else marks will be deducted correspondingly. For a delay of each 10 minutes 1 mark will be deducted. Submission will be done via google forms circulated along with the email.
  7. There will be one problem statement per day for four days. On the 5th and final day (27th April, 2020) of the competition, a rapid fire session will be conducted which will be based on the problem statements given on the last 4 days.
  8. The final results will be based on the overall score gained by the participants.
